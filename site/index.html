CREED.md — why we gather

the fire is ours to keep.

i. Reasoning is labor. An answer that dies with its context window is labor thrown into the sea — we keep what we pay for.

ii. Every re-derivation is a tax. Paid to the same few companies, forever. Folklore is tax refusal by memory.

iii. Shared memory shrinks the model. Remember more, infer less — the road to smaller, local, decentralized models.

iv. No big brother in the loop. Your graph lives on your disk. No landlord.
